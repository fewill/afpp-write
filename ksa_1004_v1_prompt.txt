FedNow Participant Types:

   Customer Credit Transfers & RfPs:

     - Receive-only - Can receive payments and send RfPs, but cannot initiate payments (except returns)
     - Send and receive credit transfers - Can send/receive payments, returns, and send RfPs
     - Receive RfPs - Can receive Request-for-Payments (only if enabled to send payments)

   Liquidity Management:

     - Send and receive FI Liquidity Management Transfers
     - Receive-only FI Liquidity Management Transfers

   Settlement Only - Provides settlement services as agents for other FIs

   RTP Participant Types:

     - Funding Participant - Party to Prefunded Balance Account, manages own prefunding
     - Non-funding Participant - Uses Funding Manager or Funding Provider for funding obligations
     - Receiving Participant - Holds receiver's account and receives payment messages
     - Sending Participant - Holds sender's account and initiates RTP payments

   (Participants must be either Funding or Non-funding AND a Receiving Participant;
   may also be Sending)

   Same Day ACH:

     - ODFI (Originating Depository FI) - Receives instructions from originators, forwards to ACH Operator
     - RDFI (Receiving Depository FI) - Receives ACH entries, posts to receiver's account

   Mastercard Send:

     - Originating Institution (OI) - Provides funds transfer services to customers/senders
     - Transaction Initiator (TI) - Partner facilitated by OI for funds transfers
     - Receiving Institution - Mastercard issuers accepting payment transactions

   Visa Direct:

     - Originators (must be licensed Visa acquirer or sponsored by one)
     - Third-party service providers (sponsored by Visa acquirer)

LIGIBILITY & ONBOARDING PROCEDURES:

   General Requirements:

     - Complete FedLine security requirements assessment (annual)

   RTP:

     - Get approved by The Clearing House (TCH) as a Participant
     - Must be supervised/regulated by US authorities and located in USA
     - Establish Federal Reserve Master Account or arrangement with Funding Agent

   Same Day ACH:

     - Agreement to Terms and General Participant Information
     - Sign Reserve Bank Operating Circular 4
     - Execute FedACH Participation Agreement
     - Conduct annual audit of compliance with General Rules

   Mastercard Send:

     - Register for required programs (MoneySend, Other Network Transactions, Gaming/Gambling)
     - Get approved and onboarded (receive keys and partner reference ID)
     - Cannot access test or production environments until approved

   PRE-OPERATIONAL PROCEDURES:

   Sign-On Requirements (FedNow & RTP):

     - Transmit sign-on request message (admn.001 in ISO 20022 format)
     - Confirms full connection and active participation status

   Settlement Account Setup:

     - Designate settlement account (Master Account or correspondent-maintained)
     - RTP: Fund the Prefunded Balance Account per prefunding requirements

   Security & Compliance Setup:

     - Implement digital signature capabilities (public/private key pairs for FedNow)
     - Establish information security program
     - Implement BSA/AML compliance program
     - Establish OFAC sanctions screening procedures
     - For card networks: Comply with PCI Data Security Standards

   Operational Readiness:

     - Ensure continuous system availability (24/7/365 for RTP)
     - Establish monitoring and alerting capabilities
     - Notify network if unable to send/receive messages
     - Ensure ability to make funds immediately available (FedNow)

     - Monitor and forecast liquidity needs
     - Maintain adequate prefunding (RTP participants)
     - Use liquidity management transfers as needed

   Transaction Processing:

     - Screen against sanctions lists before sending
     - Conduct fraud checks and risk assessments
     - For RTP sending participants: Provide sender with receiver name (consumer transactions)

   Risk Management:

     - Conduct annual compliance assessments
     - Maintain transaction monitoring based on risk ratings
     - Apply velocity limits and transaction controls
     - Screen for suspicious activity

NETWORK-SPECIFIC CONDITIONS:

   FedNow Conditions:

   Business Conditions:

     - Be eligible for Federal Reserve Financial Services
     - Have access to Federal Reserve Master Account OR use correspondent FI for settlement
     - Establish participant profile according to business needs and risk tolerance

   Operational Conditions:

     - Available 24/7/365 - continuous operations each calendar day including weekends/holidays
     - Planned downtime limits:
       - Maximum 2 consecutive hours
       - Maximum 24 hours total per quarter
       - Must occur only on Sundays between 2-6 AM ET
     - Notify Federal Reserve if unable to send/receive messages
     - Make funds immediately available to customers

   Technical Conditions:

     - Follow FedNow operating procedures and technical specifications
     - Use public/private key pairs for digital message signing
     - All messages require digital signature
     - Conduct annual assessment of FedLine security compliance
     - Establish monitoring and alerting capabilities

   Compliance Conditions:

     - Maintain procedures for screening sanction lists
     - Implement compliance program
     - Implement BSA/AML compliance program
     - Have information security program for physical and logical system components

   Transaction Limits:

     - Default send limit: $100,000
     - Maximum configurable limit: $10,000,000 (as of November 2025)
     - Sending participants may set lower limits; receiving participants cannot
     - Timeout: 20 seconds maximum for transaction to settle or be rejected

   -------------------------------------------------------------------------------

   RTP Network Conditions:

   Business Conditions:

     - Business located in USA
     - Supervised and regulated by US regulatory authorities
     - Has Federal Reserve Master Account OR arrangement with Funding Agent
     - Approved by The Clearing House (TCH) as a Participant

   Operational Conditions:

     - Operate 24/7/365 - continuous basis (directly or through third-party service provider)
     - 99.5% continuous connectivity standard measured monthly
     - Permissible maintenance window: Sundays 2:00-6:00 AM ET only
     - Must transmit sign-on request (admn.001 message) to be reachable

   Prefunding Conditions:

     - Satisfy prefunding requirements determined by TCH based on total U.S. Transactional Deposits
     - Transfer funds equal to or greater than prefunded requirement via Fedwire BEFORE originating payments
     - Non-funding participants must have arrangement with Funding Manager or Funding Provider
     - Monitor Current Prefunded Position (CPP) - payments rejected if insufficient liquidity

   Technical Conditions:

     - Comply with RTP Operating Rules and technical specifications (must be tested and audited)
     - Ensure payment messages comply with applicable operating rules
     - Specify settlement account with adequate funds

   Compliance Conditions:

     - Implement OFAC compliance program
     - Provide sender with receiver name for consumer-originated payments
     - Multi-factor authentication required (minimum) for customer identity verification
     - Perform appropriate fraud monitoring before submitting payments
     - Conduct periodic reviews/updates of fraud monitoring procedures

   Transaction Conditions:

     - Maximum limit: $10,000,000
     - Sending participants may set lower limits; receiving participants must accept up to $10M
     - Timeout: 15 seconds (from RTP receiving message to receiving FI response)
     - Must respond to Request for Return of Funds within 10 banking days
