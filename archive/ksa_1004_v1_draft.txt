KSA 1004: Types and Functions of Participation
Draft Test Questions

Q1 — Factual

Which action can a FedNow receive-only participant perform without the ability to initiate credit transfer payments?

A. Send a Request for Payment
B. Originate a customer credit transfer
C. Initiate a liquidity management transfer

Answer: A

Source: 2026 AFPP Handbook, FedNow participant types section, approximately line 3031.
"Receive-only participants have the ability to send RfPs but are not eligible to receive RfPs
because they cannot initiate customer credit transfers as a response to a payment request."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q2 — Factual

Which combination of participant roles is required of all participants in the RTP network?

A. Sending Participant and Receiving Participant
B. Funding or Non-funding Participant and Receiving Participant
C. Funding Participant and Non-funding Participant

Answer: B

Source: 2026 AFPP Handbook, RTP participant types section, approximately line 3071.
"Any participant must be (i) either a Funding Participant or a Non-funding Participant, and
(ii) a Receiving Participant. A participant also may be a Sending Participant."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q3 — Factual

When a financial institution participates as a receive-only participant in the FedNow network,
which message type is it required to receive?

A. pacs.002 (Payment Status Report)
B. pacs.004 (Payment Return)
C. pacs.008 (Customer Credit Transfer)

Answer: C

Source: 2026 AFPP Handbook, Table 22: Message Types by Participant – FedNow, approximately
lines 3564–3617. For the receive-only participant type, pacs.008 is listed as Prohibited to send
and Mandatory to receive.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q4 — Conditional

Which role must a financial institution also agree to fulfill when it originates ACH entries in
the Same Day ACH network?

A. ACH Operator
B. Receiving Depository Financial Institution
C. Third-party sender

Answer: B

Source: 2026 AFPP Handbook, Same Day ACH participant types section, approximately line 3080.
"An FI may participate in the ACH Network as an RDFI without also being an ODFI; however,
if an FI chooses to originate ACH entries, it must also agree to act as an RDFI."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q5 — Procedural

Which message type must an RTP participant transmit to confirm full connection and active
participation status on the network?

A. admn.001
B. pacs.002
C. pacs.008

Answer: A

Source: 2026 AFPP Handbook, RTP requirements prior to receiving/originating transactions,
approximately line 3985. "Participants must first transmit a sign-on request (ISO 20022 message
ID: admn.001) message to be reachable on the RTP Network."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q6 — Conditional

Under what condition will the RTP network reject a payment submitted by a sending participant?

A. The sending participant has not established a Federal Reserve Master Account
B. The receiving participant's internally configured transaction limit is set below the payment amount
C. The sending participant's Current Prefunded Position is insufficient to cover the payment

Answer: C

Source: 2026 AFPP Handbook, Reasons for implementing prefunding requirements, approximately
line 8165. "RTP verifies the sending participant's Current Prefunded Position (CPP). RTP will
reject the payment if the sending participant has insufficient liquidity to cover the payment."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q7 — Conditional

Under what condition may a third-party service provider participate as an originator in the
Visa Direct network?

A. The third-party service provider has established a direct settlement account with Visa
B. The third-party service provider is sponsored by a licensed Visa acquirer
C. The third-party service provider has completed registration for the Visa Direct program

Answer: B

Source: 2026 AFPP Handbook, Visa Direct participant types section, approximately line 3142.
"Third-party service providers who offer push payment services to end users (consumers or
businesses) can be sponsored by a Visa licensed acquirer to offer push payment capabilities
to Visa card accounts."
